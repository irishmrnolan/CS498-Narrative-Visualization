CS498 Data Visualization

Welcome to my Narrative Visualization for CS498 Data Visualization. In the following pages we will be exploring data about World Development Indicators. The dataset can be found at wdi.worldbank.org . For the sake of this project, I have limited my analysis to 10 countries. We will be looking at population, gross domestic product(GDP), and life expectancy. On each of the following pages you be presented with high level data with the ability to explore predesigned low level data. Enjoy!
